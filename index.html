About Me

I’ve always been drawn to the elegance of math and the thrill of curiosity — and theoretical neuroscience brings them together beautifully. In my recent project, supervised by Peter Latham, I’m focused on constructing a biologically plausible mathematical model of memory with sufficient capacity to explain human memory!
